Chicken Stew

Light Kerala-style chicken stew with coconut milk.

Prep 15 min
Cook 20-25 min
Servings Father – 100g chicken; Mother – 125g chicken; Daughter – 125g chicken; Son – 150g chicken
chicken stew
Visual reference only

Ingredients

  • 400-500g skinless chicken
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1-2 potatoes (optional)
  • 1 cup thin coconut milk
  • ½ cup thick coconut milk
  • 1-inch ginger (julienned)
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • ½ tsp pepper
  • Whole spices (cinnamon, cardamom, cloves)
  • Curry leaves
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt

Health Note

Use max 1 tbsp oil. Minimal potatoes for heart health. Skinless chicken keeps fat low.

Benefits

High protein supports recovery. Coconut milk offers healthy fats for brain & immunity.

Instructions

5 steps
  1. 1
    Heat oil, add spices, ginger, chilies, onion sauté.
  2. 2
    Add chicken, pepper, salt, cook 5 minutes.
  3. 3
    Add thin coconut milk, potato, simmer till tender.
  4. 4
    Add thick coconut milk, simmer 2-3 min (no boil).
  5. 5
    Garnish with curry leaves.
