Oats with Fruits Porridge

Nutritious oats porridge topped with fresh fruits and nuts.

Prep 5 min
Cook 10 min
Servings Father – 1 bowl; Mother – 1 bowl; Daughter – 1 bowl; Son – 1.5 bowls
oats fruits porridge breakfast
Visual reference only

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats
  • 2 cups milk or water
  • ½ cup mixed fruits (banana, apple, berries)
  • 1 tbsp nuts and seeds (optional)
  • Honey or jaggery to taste

Health Note

Father: Use minimal sweetener. Son: Moderate sweetener allowed.

Benefits

Provides vitamins, fiber, and healthy fats. Balanced energy for the day.

Instructions

3 steps
  1. 1
    Cook oats in milk or water till soft.
  2. 2
    Top with chopped fruits and nuts.
  3. 3
    Sweeten with honey/jaggery if desired.
